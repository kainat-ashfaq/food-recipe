BIRYANI RECIPE

Ingredients

"Basmati rice", "Chicken", "Yogurt", "Fried onions", "Tomatoes", "Garlic", "Ginger", "Green chilies", "Biryani masala", "Coriander", "Mint", "Saffron", "Milk", "Ghee", "Whole spices (cinnamon, cloves, bay leaves)",

  • Recipe
  • Soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes, then boil it with whole spices until it's 70% cooked.
  • In a large pot, heat ghee and add chicken pieces. Cook until browned.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste, biryani masala, and yogurt. Cook until the oil separates.
  • Add tomatoes, fried onions, green chilies, coriander, and mint. Cook until the chicken is tender.
  • Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken. Sprinkle saffron-soaked milk on top.
  • Cover the pot with a tight lid and cook on low heat (dum) for 20-25 minutes.
  • Mix gently before serving to combine the flavors and layers.

PULAO RECIPE

Ingredients

"Basmati rice", "Chicken or mutton", "Onions", "Garlic", "Ginger", "Yogurt", "Green chilies", "Cumin seeds", "Coriander", "Mint", "Whole spices (cinnamon, cloves, bay leaves)", "Ghee",

  • RECIPE
  • Soak basmati rice for 30 minutes, then drain.
  • In a pot, heat ghee and fry whole spices and onions until golden.
  • Add garlic-ginger paste and sauté until fragrant.
  • Add chicken or mutton, cook until browned.
  • Add yogurt, green chilies, coriander, and mint. Cook until the meat is tender.
  • Add the soaked rice and enough water to cook the rice.
  • Cover and cook on low heat until the rice is fully cooked and fluffy.
  • Garnish with fried onions and serve hot.

DAAL CHAWAL RECIPE

Ingredients

"Basmati rice", "Split lentils (daal)", "Onions", "Tomatoes", "Garlic", "Ginger", "Turmeric", "Cumin seeds", "Coriander", "Green chilies", "Ghee",

  • RECIPE
  • Cook basmati rice in water until tender, then drain and set aside.
  • In a separate pot, heat ghee and fry onions until golden.
  • Add garlic, ginger, turmeric, cumin seeds, and cook until fragrant.
  • Add split lentils and water. Cook until the lentils are soft and fully cooked.
  • Add tomatoes, green chilies, and coriander. Cook until well combined.
  • Serve the daal over the rice, garnished with fried onions and coriander.

KHAUSA RECIPE

Ingredients

"Egg noodles", "Chicken", "Coconut milk", "Onions", "Garlic", "Ginger", "Turmeric", "Curry powder", "Chickpea flour", "Green chilies", "Lemon", "Coriander", "Fried onions", "Ghee",

  • RECIPE
  • Boil egg noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  • In a pot, heat ghee and fry onions until golden.
  • Add garlic, ginger, turmeric, and curry powder. Sauté until fragrant.
  • Add chicken pieces and cook until browned.
  • Stir in chickpea flour and cook for a few minutes.
  • Add coconut milk and water, bring to a boil, then simmer until the chicken is tender.
  • Serve the chicken curry over the noodles, garnished with fried onions, coriander, and a squeeze of lemon.

CHICKEN kARAHI RECIPE

Ingredients

"Chicken", "Tomatoes", "Onions", "Garlic", "Ginger", "Green chilies", "Coriander", "Cumin seeds", "Red chili powder", "Garam masala", "Ghee",

  • RECIPE
  • In a karahi (wok), heat ghee and fry onions until golden.
  • Add garlic-ginger paste and sauté until fragrant.
  • Add chicken pieces and cook until browned.
  • Add chopped tomatoes, green chilies, coriander, cumin seeds, and red chili powder.
  • Cook on high heat until the tomatoes are soft and the chicken is tender.
  • Add garam masala and cook for a few more minutes.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or roti.

NIHARI RECIPE

Ingredients

"Beef shank", "Nihari masala", "Wheat flour", "Ginger", "Garlic", "Fried onions", "Lemon", "Coriander", "Green chilies", "Ghee",

  • RECIPE
  • In a large pot, heat ghee and fry onions until golden.
  • Add garlic-ginger paste and sauté.
  • Add beef shank pieces and cook until browned.
  • Add nihari masala and cook until fragrant.
  • Add water and cook on low heat for several hours until the meat is tender.
  • Mix wheat flour with water to make a slurry and add it to the nihari to thicken.
  • Garnish with ginger, lemon wedges, coriander, and green chilies before serving.
