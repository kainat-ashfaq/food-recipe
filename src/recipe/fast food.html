CHICKEN PIZZA RECIPE

Ingredients

Pizza dough", "Tomato sauce", "Mozzarella cheese", "Grilled chicken", "Bell peppers", "Onions", "Oregano

  • Recipe
  • Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  • Roll out the pizza dough on a baking sheet.
  • Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  • Slice the grilled chicken into thin strips and scatter them over the sauce.
  • Add sliced bell peppers and onions on top.
  • Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and oregano.
  • Bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes, until the cheese is melted and the crust is crispy.
  • Let it cool slightly, then cut into slices and enjoy.

BBQ PIZZA RECIPE

Ingredients

"Pizza dough", "BBQ sauce", "Mozzarella cheese", "Grilled chicken", "Red onions", "Cilantro"

  • RECIPE
  • Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  • Roll out the pizza dough on a baking sheet.
  • Spread a layer of BBQ sauce over the dough.
  • Add sliced grilled chicken evenly across the pizza.
  • Top with thinly sliced red onions.
  • Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the entire pizza.
  • Bake for 12-15 minutes, until the cheese is bubbly and the crust is golden.
  • Remove from the oven and sprinkle with fresh cilantro before serving.

TIKKA PIZZA RECIPE

Ingredients

"Pizza dough", "Tomato sauce", "Mozzarella cheese", "Tikka marinated chicken", "Onions", "Green chilies", "Coriander"

  • RECIPE

CHEESE BURGER RECIPE

Ingredients

"Burger bun", "Beef patty", "Cheddar cheese", "Lettuce", "Tomato", "Onions", "Pickles", "Ketchup", "Mustard"

  • RECIPE
  • Preheat a grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  • Season the beef patty with salt and pepper, then cook for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until done to your liking.
  • Place a slice of cheddar cheese on the patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  • Toast the burger bun lightly on the grill or skillet.
  • Assemble the burger: Spread ketchup and mustard on the bottom bun, add lettuce, tomato, and pickles.
  • Place the beef patty with melted cheese on top.
  • Add sliced onions, then top with the other half of the bun.
  • Serve immediately with your favorite sides.

CHICKEN BURGER RECIPE

Ingredients

"Burger bun", "Grilled chicken breast", "Lettuce", "Tomato", "Onions", "Pickles", "Mayo"

  • RECIPE
  • Preheat a grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  • Season the chicken breast with salt, pepper, and your choice of spices.
  • Grill the chicken breast for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked.
  • Toast the burger bun lightly on the grill or skillet.
  • Assemble the burger: Spread mayo on the bottom bun, add lettuce, tomato, and pickles.
  • Place the grilled chicken breast on top.
  • Add sliced onions, then top with the other half of the bun.
  • Serve with your favorite side dish.

CHICKEN ROLL RECIPE

Ingredients

"Paratha or tortilla", "BBQ chicken", "Onions", "Green chutney", "Cabbage", "Yogurt"

  • RECIPE
  • Warm the paratha or tortilla on a skillet until soft and pliable.
  • Spread a layer of mayo over the center of the paratha.
  • Add slices of grilled chicken on top.
  • Sprinkle sliced onions and shredded cabbage over the chicken.
  • Drizzle some green chutney on top.
  • Roll the paratha tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  • Serve hot, with dipping sauce on the side.
